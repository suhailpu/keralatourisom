PALAKKAD

Palakkad also known as Palghat, is a city and municipality in the state of Kerala in India, spread over an area of 26.60 km2; it is the administrative headquarters of the Palakkad District. The city is situated about 350 kilometres (217 mi) north of state capital Thiruvananthapuram and 50 kilometers south west of Coimbatore in Tamil Nadu, and 66 km north east of Thrissur on the Salem-Kanyakumari National Highway NH 544

Tourist attractions

Main tourist attraction is Palakkad fort in the heart of the city and Malampuzha Dam and Malampuzha gardens (15 km away). Near Malampuzha gardens are Fantasy park (a water theme amusement park), a ropeway, snake park and a beautiful aquarium.Malampuzha also has South India's first rock garden[citation needed]. Ahalia Heritage village is an attraction near Kanjikode. Kalpathy Heritage village is the main tourist attraction in the heart of the city. Numerous temples like Hemambika temple, Kodumbu temple, Manappullikkavu Devi temple etc attract tourists too.
